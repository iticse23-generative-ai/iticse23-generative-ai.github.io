The Australasian Computing Education Conference (ACE) is a conference on research, teaching, and innovation in computing education in its various aspects, at all levels, and in all contexts. ACE is the only Australasian conference devoted entirely to education in Computing.

Information for Presenters

In 2023, the formal presentation of research papers is scheduled for 15 minutes, followed by 5 minutes of discussion. The ACE community encourages discussion of research presented, and we strongly encourage those discussions to be continued during the coffee and lunch breaks.

Information for Session Chairs

Session chairs should familiarize themselves with the papers in the session they are chairing. Prior to the start of the session, they should identify the author who will be presenting each paper and ensure that any slides are uploaded to the appropriate location. The session chair is responsible for introducing each speaker and for guiding the discussion phase after each presentation.
